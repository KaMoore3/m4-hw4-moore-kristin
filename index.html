The Beginning

According to Wikipedia Stone Sour was founded by Corey Taylor and Joel Ekman who took their bands name from a coctail menu at one of their local bars. The rest is history

Where They Are Now

Currently Stone Sour is on an indefinite hiatus which came after their last album Hydrograd. Band members are currently doing individual work.

Personal Struggles

The lead singer and founder Corey Taylor had struggled personally although through his work not only with Stone Sour but also with his other bands. Failed marraiges, struggles staying sober and mental health issues have been a persistent issue.
